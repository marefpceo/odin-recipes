Curry Sauce

Description

Easy curry sauce recipe.
Pairs great with shrimp or chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 cup whole milk or coconut milk

Steps

  1. Rub together butter and flour until mixed. Stir in curry powder.
    Melt this slowly in milk, stirring until dissolved to avoid lumps.
    (If lumps happen, process in a blender until smooth)
  2. Use this very basic and easy to prepare sauce to heat cooked and cleaned
    shrimp, lobster or crab meat (cooked - both imitation and real) or chicken.
  3. Taste and add salt and pepper, as needed (the full amount of the salt may not be required).
  4. This is excellent for dressing up leftovers or for using when making casseroles.
