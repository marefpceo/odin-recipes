Cajun Chicken

Description

Cajun-style grilled chicken breasts for varied meals such as Caesar salad, sandwiches,
and a dinner entree! Cajun Chicken can be served hot or cold.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp Cajun seasoning
  • 2 tbsp dried italian-style seasoning
  • garlic powder to taste
  • 2 tbsp lemon pepper
  • 10 skinless, boneless chicken breat halves - pounded
    to 1/2 inch thickness

Steps

  1. In a large shallow dish, mix the oil, Cajun seasoning, Italian seasoning, garlic powder,
    and lemon pepper. Place the chicken in the dish, and turn to coat with the mixture.
    Cover, and refrigerate for 1/2 hour.
  2. Preheat the grill for high heat.
  3. Lightly oil the grill grate. Drain chicken, and discard marinade. Place chicken on hot
    grill and cook for 6 to 8 minutes on each side, or until juices run clear.
